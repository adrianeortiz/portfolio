Who am I?

I'm a lifelong learner and I love how with code and various cloud tools you can automate tedious tasks, increase security, stability, and build scalable solutions that can be easily accessible to millions of people who have access to the internet. I enjoy the challenge that DevOps presents because it allows me to continually learn and stay up to date with new technologies and to then utilize that knowledge to solve difficult problems.

With my background in Information Systems, I began my career in I.T. help desk. This position allowed me to gain useful soft skills as well as the technical skills needed to do an initial career switch to QA. I subsequently enrolled in TechWorld with Nana, a fully online DevOps bootcamp, in order to further enhance my skills and make another career switch to DevOps / SRE. I have gained skills in various languages and frameworks such as Python, Docker, Kubernetes, Jenkins, Ansible, and Terraform. I have integrated these skills into various cloud based projects that are highlighted here on my portfolio, and GitHub profile.
